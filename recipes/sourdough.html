Sourdough

Description

Easy, beginner-friendly sourdough bread recipe that looks and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 50 g sourdough starter
  • 500 g flour (some combination of white and wheat)
  • 350 g water
  • 10 g salt

Steps

  1. Mix the sourdough starter, flour, water, and flour together in a bowl.
  2. Cover the bowl and let sit for 1 hour.
  3. Stretch and fold the dough over itself. Rotate the dough a quarter turn at a time and repeat until the dough forms into a tight ball.
  4. Cover the bowl and let sit for 30 minutes, then repeat step 3.
  5. Cover the bowl and let sit for 5-8 hours until the dough has risen roughly by 50%.
  6. Shape the dough into a tight ball on a lightly floured surface. Place the dough seam-side down into a lighly floured dutch oven and let sit for another 1-2 hours.
  7. Score the top of the dough and place the covered dutch oven into a preheated oven at 450 degrees F.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes, then remove the dutch oven cover.
  9. Bake for another 30 minutes or until the crust is a golden brown.
  10. Cool the bread for at least 1 hour until slicing.
